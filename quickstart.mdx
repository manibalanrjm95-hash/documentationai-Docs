## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An active email address for account verification
- 5 minutes of time

</Callout>

## Create Your Account and First Space

Follow these steps to sign up, verify your account, create a documentation space, and add your first document.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit [https://mani-balan.com/signup](https://mani-balan.com/signup) and choose your preferred method.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">

        Enter your email and password, then click **Create Account**.

      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Google" icon="chrome">

        Click **Continue with Google** and authorize the app.

      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="check-circle">

    Check your inbox for a verification email from Mani Balan. Click the link to activate your account.

    <Callout kind="tip">

      Didn't receive it? Check your spam folder or request a new one from the dashboard.

    </Callout>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Documentation Space" icon="folder-plus">

    Log in at [https://app.mani-balan.com](https://app.mani-balan.com).

    Click **New Space** in the sidebar, enter a name like `My Project Docs`, and select **Create**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Document" icon="file-plus">

    In your new space, click **New Document**. Name it `Quickstart.mdx` and paste this sample content:

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
```mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: My first document.
---

## Hello World

This is your first Mani Balan document!
```
```markdown
# Welcome

This is your first document in Markdown format.
```
    </CodeGroup>

    Click **Publish** to make it live.

  </Step>
</Steps>
