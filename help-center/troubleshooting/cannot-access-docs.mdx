title: Cannot Access Documentation
description: Resolve access issues with mani balan docs.
---

## Symptoms
- Unable to open your project docs
- "Access Denied" message appears

## Possible Causes
- Not logged in
- Insufficient permissions

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    ````
    Go to https://manibalan.com and make sure you are signed in.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request access">
    ````
    Ask the project owner to invite your email as a collaborator.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
